=== Flickr Comment Importer ===
Contributors: donncha
Tags: flickr,comments
Tested up to: 4.1.1
Stable tag: 0.3
Requires at least: 4.0

Import comments from your Flickr Stream into your blog posts of the same name.

Go to the settings page at Settings->Flickr Comments to configure the plugin.
